Indian-Spiced Tomato and Yogurt Curry with Blueberries

Ingredients:
  • AH Snoepgroente tomaat - 250g
  • AH Romaatjes - 250g
  • Campina Halfvolle yoghurt - 500ml
  • AH Blauwe bessen - 150g
Instructions:
  1. 1. Wash and roughly chop the AH Snoepgroente tomaat and AH Romaatjes. Set aside.
  2. 2. In a pan, heat a teaspoon of oil (or use a non-stick pan) and saut√© the chopped tomatoes until they form a soft paste, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. 3. Add Indian spices like turmeric, cumin, and coriander (use pantry staples if available) to the tomato paste, and cook for 2 more minutes.
  4. 4. Stir in 200ml of Campina Halfvolle yoghurt gradually, mixing well to avoid curdling. Simmer on low heat for 5 minutes.
  5. 5. Add 50g of AH Blauwe bessen for a unique sweet-tart flavor, and cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. 6. Serve warm as a side or with a chapati or rice if available from your pantry. Use any leftover tomatoes or blueberries in a salad to avoid waste.

Yogurt and Tomato Raita Salad

Ingredients:
  • AH Snoepgroente tomaat - 200g
  • Campina Volle yoghurt - 400ml
  • AH Romaatjes - 200g
Instructions:
  1. 1. Finely chop the AH Snoepgroente tomaat and AH Romaatjes.
  2. 2. In a bowl, mix 400ml of Campina Volle yoghurt with the chopped tomatoes.
  3. 3. Add a pinch of cumin powder, salt, and chopped cilantro (if available in your pantry) for an Indian touch.
  4. 4. Chill for 10 minutes in the refrigerator if desired, or serve immediately as a side dish or light meal.
  5. 5. Use any leftover tomatoes for a quick salsa or soup base to minimize waste.

Blueberry and Yogurt Power Bowl

Ingredients:
  • AH Blauwe bessen - 200g
  • Campina Halfvolle yoghurt - 500ml
Instructions:
  1. 1. Take 200g of AH Blauwe bessen and rinse them well under cold water.
  2. 2. In a bowl, add 250ml of Campina Halfvolle yoghurt as the base.
  3. 3. Top the yogurt with the blueberries for a fiber and antioxidant boost.
  4. 4. Sprinkle a pinch of cardamom or cinnamon (if available in your pantry) for an Indian flavor twist.
  5. 5. Serve immediately as a light meal. Use any leftover yogurt for a smoothie or raita, and extra blueberries can be frozen for future use.
