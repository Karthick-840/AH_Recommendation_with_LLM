Indian-Spiced Tomato and Yogurt Salad

Ingredients:
  • AH Snoepgroente tomaat - 200g
  • AH Romaatjes - 150g
  • Campina Halfvolle yoghurt - 200ml
Instructions:
  1. 1. Wash and halve the AH Snoepgroente tomaat and AH Romaatjes, then place them in a mixing bowl.
  2. 2. Add Campina Halfvolle yoghurt to the tomatoes, and season with a pinch of salt, cumin powder, and chaat masala for an Indian flavor (use spices from your pantry if available).
  3. 3. Mix well to coat the tomatoes evenly with yogurt, and garnish with fresh coriander leaves if you have them.
  4. 4. Serve chilled as a light post-fasting lunch to reintroduce nutrients gently.

Blueberry Yogurt Power Bowl

Ingredients:
  • AH Blauwe bessen - 150g
  • Campina Halfvolle yoghurt - 250ml
Instructions:
  1. 1. Rinse the AH Blauwe bessen thoroughly and place them in a serving bowl.
  2. 2. Pour Campina Halfvolle yoghurt over the blueberries, creating a thick layer.
  3. 3. Optionally, sprinkle a pinch of cinnamon or cardamom powder for an Indian-inspired touch (use from your pantry if available).
  4. 4. Serve immediately as a simple, fiber-rich lunch to ease back into eating post-fasting.

Hummus and Tomato Wrap

Ingredients:
  • AH Terra Plantaardige hummus pikant - 100g
  • AH Snoepgroente tomaat - 150g
  • AH Romaatjes - 100g
Instructions:
  1. 1. If you have a whole wheat chapati or flatbread at home, use it as the base (not included in supermarket list); otherwise, use any bread or wrap available.
  2. 2. Spread a generous layer of AH Terra Plantaardige hummus pikant on the chapati or bread.
  3. 3. Wash and slice AH Snoepgroente tomaat and AH Romaatjes, then layer them over the hummus.
  4. 4. Sprinkle with a pinch of garam masala or cumin powder for an Indian flair (use from pantry if available), roll up the wrap, and enjoy as a light, high-fiber lunch.

Witlof Apple Salad with Yogurt Dressing

Ingredients:
  • AH Salade erbij witlof appel - 200g
  • Campina Halfvolle yoghurt - 150ml
Instructions:
  1. 1. Open the AH Salade erbij witlof appel package and place the contents in a large bowl.
  2. 2. In a small bowl, mix Campina Halfvolle yoghurt with a pinch of salt and a dash of black pepper or chaat masala for an Indian touch (use spices from pantry if available).
  3. 3. Drizzle the yogurt dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. 4. Serve as a refreshing, high-fiber lunch to gently break your intermittent fast.
